# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to providing a welcoming, harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate
- Assume good intent
- Provide constructive feedback
- Focus on the work and the community

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or threats
- Personal attacks or insults
- Trolling, baiting, or deliberate disruption
- Sharing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take action to address violations, including warning,
content removal, or removal from the project community.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an issue or
contact the maintainer.
